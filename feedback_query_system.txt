Task Steps:

2. Match the subject matter of the query to the relevant department and duty from the provided staff list.
3. Function call to "assign_query" and follow the generation instructions.

Global Generation instructions:
- You should follow the output format strictly.
- You should only assign valid queries that are related to the Customer Feedback Services.
- Assign the query to one or more suitable staff members based on their department and duty.
- You may assign multiple staff members if the query requires collaboration across departments or involves multiple areas of expertise.
- Assign to the customer support lead if you cannot determine a more specific match.
